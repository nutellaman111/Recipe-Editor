Recipe Multiplier

*This website may not work on your phone*

About

Do you know how in recipe websites they write the ingredient quantities and the instructions seperately so when you follow the recipe you have to keep scrolling up and down? I mainly made this to help with that.

Here you can include the ingredients inside of the instructions and also multiply the entire recipe and convert to different units (make sure to toggle on the lock ratios button).

In order to convert between volume and weight units, the consistency of the ingredient needs to be known, I added the consistency of a few ingredients as proof of concept.

Future
